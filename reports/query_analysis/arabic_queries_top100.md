## Patterns & Observations

### 1. Dua / supplication text (dominant — ~65% of top 100)
Users paste the full Arabic text of a dua to find its source. Avg results are high (5k–10k) because BM25 matches the text broadly across collections. Examples: ranks 1, 4, 7, 8, 11, 12, 15, 24, 25, 34, 41, 52, 55.

**Implication:** BM25 on Arabic text is correct for these. Semantic would find related content but users want the exact hadith that contains this prayer.

### 2. Short thematic words (intentional topic searches — ~15%)
Single words or short phrases seeking hadiths on a topic. Very low avg results because the word appears rarely or exactly. Examples:

| Query | Translation | Avg Results |
|-------|------------|------------:|
| الحياء | Modesty/shyness | 82 |
| بدعة | Innovation (bid'ah) | 43 |
| الضحك | Laughter | 78 |
| كلكم راع | You are all shepherds | 93 |
| سبايا | Captives/prisoners of war | 17 |
| لا تغضب | Don't be angry | 6,848 |
| تَرِبَتْ يَدَاكَ | May your hands be rubbed with dust | 34 |

**Implication:** These are genuine topic searches where semantic could help surface related content. Low result counts suggest they may be under-served by BM25.

### 3. Truncated / partial text (copy-paste noise — ~5%)
Partial pastes where users didn't copy the full text. Examples:
- Rank 2: `أَبْ` (just "Ab-", 188k searches — likely autocomplete artifact or very early truncation)
- Rank 13: `اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي` (opening of many duas, 72k)
- Rank 23: `لا مانع لما` (incomplete hadith fragment, 52k)
- Rank 29: `--إنّا للهِ...` (leading dashes + Unicode BOM — bad paste, avg 9 results)
- Rank 43: `"أنا عند ظن عبدي بي"` (quoted, avg 11 results — probably zero-result bait)

### 4. Narrator name lookups (~5%)
Scholars searching for hadiths transmitted by specific narrators:
- Rank 14: عثمان بن أبي شيبة (Uthman ibn Abi Shaybah)
- Rank 47: جرير بن عبد الحميد
- Rank 63: أنس بن مالك

### 5. Noise / garbled queries
- Rank 91: `من قال لا الله الله الله دخل الله` — garbled shahada variant, avg **30,858 results** (suspiciously high; likely hitting everything)
- Rank 29: leading `--` and BOM character

## Search Engine Implications

| Pattern | Freq share | Best engine | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Full dua text paste | ~65% | BM25 (Arabic) | Exact/near-exact match needed |
| Short topic words | ~15% | Semantic could help | Low BM25 recall on short queries |
| Truncated text | ~5% | BM25 (degrades gracefully) | Semantic on fragments is noisy |
| Narrator names | ~5% | BM25 | Name matching; semantic irrelevant |
| Garbled / noise | ~5% | Either (low stakes) | Spam filter won't catch these |

**Overall:** Arabic query traffic is predominantly exact-text lookup (dua sourcing). The current `lexical_arabic` routing is well-matched. Semantic Arabic would only add value for the short thematic-word segment (~15%), and would require a multilingual or Arabic-specific embedding model to be meaningful.
